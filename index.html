KEEP THEM BEAUTIFUL

Nail Care Guide

Your manicure is an investment. Follow these tips to preserve your nails for 2–3 weeks.

First 24–48 Hours
Avoid submerging your hands in water. No dishwashing, long showers without gloves, or hot baths. Let the gel fully cure.
Critical curing time
Daily Cuticle Care
Apply cuticle oil or balm 2–3 times a day, especially before bed. Healthy cuticles = longer-lasting sets.
Hydration = longevity
Wear Gloves
Use rubber gloves for dishwashing, cleaning, gardening, or any chore involving water or chemicals.
Protect your investment
Never Pick or Peel
Peeling damages your natural nail bed. If anything does peel, book a professional removal or fillling with me instead.
Be patient
Don’t Use as Tools
Avoid opening cans, scraping stickers, or typing aggressively. Treat your nails like the luxury they are.
Gentle is better
Schedule Your Fill
Book every 2–3 weeks for a fill or a new set. This keeps your manicure strong and prevents lifting.
Maintenance is everything

Pro Tip: If you notice lifting, cracking, or any discomfort, message me right away. Never attempt DIY removal with acetone or tools. Your natural nails will thank you.
